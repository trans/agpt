AGPT recipe + builder-equivalence validation (2026-04-26)

The Gutenberg-5M direct-AGPT result of PPL ~25 (vs wrap-around's 6.78)
prompted a check: is the new corpus-radix builder training-equivalent
to the old StreamingRadixBuilder, and is the recipe still healthy?

## Builder equivalence

The new corpus-radix builder produces bit-equivalent record COUNTS
(verified earlier: 1,666,215 nodes; 27,046,236 edge chars on Shakespeare
d=32) but different within-depth record ORDERING (per-root-char grouping
vs interleaved BFS).

The training-time PPL difference between builders:
- 3SE linear: ~1 PPL (new worse)
- 5SE linear: ~0.7 PPL (new better)
- 5SE off: ~0.06 PPL (essentially identical)

Different ordering → different convergence trajectory but same general
regime. Within the noise band of the recipe overall (~1-3 PPL run-to-run
variation in past sweeps). Conclusion: new builder is not broken.

## 5SE > 3SE for linear-mass-weight Shakespeare → overtraining

| Epochs | Old linear | New linear |
|--------|-----------:|-----------:|
| 3      |      12.85 |      13.81 |
| 5      |      15.66 |      14.95 |

Bumping epochs at this recipe HURTS PPL on Shakespeare. So the implicit
plan of "scale recipe by adding epochs" doesn't transfer cleanly.

## Implication for Gutenberg 5M result

| Setup                                | Wall-clock | PPL    |
|--------------------------------------|-----------:|-------:|
| Wrap-around-via-synth (10k SGD)      |      ~5min |   6.78 |
| AGPT-direct 3SE linear (this builder)|       4min |  25.81 |
| AGPT-direct 5SE linear               |       7min |  24.89 |
| AGPT-direct 5SE off                  |       7min |  19.76 |

Even with mass-weight off (the better config on Shakespeare 5SE), the
gap to wrap-around stays ~3x. AGPT-direct on 5M corpus needs more than
just "5SE instead of 3" to compete — likely a meaningful recipe rework
(new lr schedule, possibly different optimizer or sampler).

The wrap-around-via-synth approach scales naturally because it reuses
the well-tuned SGD-window recipe over a richer (synth) corpus, getting
10k optimizer steps vs AGPT-direct's ~325 (5SE × 65 subtrees).
